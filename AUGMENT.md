## Augmentations Applied

1. Gaussian Noise: sigma randomly sampled from [5, 20]
2. JPEG Compression: Quality randomly sampled from [20, 80]
3. DPI Downsampling: Scaled to simulate 150 DPI or 72 DPI
4. Random Cropping: 1–3% removed from each border
5. Bit-Depth Reduction: 8-bit grayscale reduced to 4-bit
